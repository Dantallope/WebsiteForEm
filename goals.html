Goals

  • Short-Term Goals

    In the short term, my goal is to continue developing as a coach and leader, focusing on strengthening my ability to motivate and guide athletes to achieve their personal and team goals. I have officially taken the head coach position at the high school team that I have been coaching for 5 years, and I hope to help rebuild the program into something that can be as successful as the years before it. I aim to build a strong, cohesive team culture that prioritizes discipline, teamwork, and continuous improvement. I also want to refine my skills in communication and conflict resolution to create an even more supportive environment where every individual - parents, admin, and athletes - feels heard and valued. Additionally, I plan to focus on expanding my network and gaining further professional development opportunities to enhance my expertise in both coaching and leadership.

  • Long-Term Goals

    In the long term, my goal is to work for the Department of Education, where I can help shape educational policies and programs that support both students and educators. By 2026, I aim to take on leadership roles that allow me to influence positive change on a larger scale, ensuring that education systems are accessible, effective, and empowering. I plan to continue my education and certifications to deepen my expertise in educational leadership and policy development. Ultimately, I want to contribute to creating an inclusive, growth-oriented educational environment where all students, parents and educators have the resources and support they need to succeed.
