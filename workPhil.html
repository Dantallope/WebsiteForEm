Work Philosophy

  • I believe that effective leadership is grounded in purpose, and the best way to inspire others is through intentional action. Whether I'm coaching a swim team, collaborating with colleagues, or handling individual tasks, I focus on clear goals and maintaining a strong work ethic. I lead by example, knowing that every action I take has an impact on those around me.

    Through my coaching experience and my time at Starbucks, I’ve learned the importance of creating supportive, team-oriented environments where individuals feel empowered to grow and excel. Both roles taught me the value of communication, resilience, and maintaining a positive attitude, especially in high-pressure situations. I’ve learned how to balance leading with empathy and holding others accountable, ensuring that everyone feels valued and motivated to contribute their best.

    I believe that great leadership involves setting clear expectations and holding others accountable while also offering the support needed to help them succeed. I foster an atmosphere of open communication, where individuals are encouraged to take ownership of their work and grow from both their successes and challenges. By leading with purpose and inspiring through action, I believe we can achieve personal growth, collective success, and create an environment where everyone thrives.
